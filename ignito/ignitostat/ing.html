About Ignito Corporation

Established in 2025 as a sole proprietorship, Ignito is officially registered under the Indian MSME scheme.

Our flagship project — the Smart LPG Distribution App — is designed to simplify and automate the LPG delivery process by enabling digital record-keeping, tracking, and workforce management for distributors.

As a startup, our core focus is bringing digital solutions to real-world problems. We are committed to building tools that contribute to the broader goal of India's digital future.

Core Focus

  • Smart Automation Streamlining complex workflows into simple, automated steps.
  • Accessible Platforms Intuitive interfaces designed for users of all technical backgrounds.
  • Scalable Growth Building robust solutions that grow seamlessly with your business.

Project Roadmap

The journey of building the Smart LPG Distribution App from concept to deployment.

Phase 1

Ideation & Research

Understanding the LPG distribution system and identifying core gaps in current processes.

Phase 2

Design & Development

Creating the Smart LPG Distribution App with intuitive role-based access controls.

Phase 3

Testing & Trials

Running internal trials to ensure tracking accuracy and maximum operational efficiency.

Phase 4

Integration

Seamless subscription management and monetization via secure Razorpay integration.

Phase 5

Market Expansion

Enhancing with user feedback, scaling features, and expanding the internal team.
